Our Mission


VISION:

To establish an organization that is capable of eradicating the illiteracy which is due to poverty, i.e. lack of literacy due to economic status; further, identifying bright minds and enabling them to excel in their field of interest simultaneously monitoring their academic activities throughout their career..

GOAL:

• Provide sound basic and moral education to the deprived.
• Organising Parents-Teacher meet on a frequent basis, thus allowing guardians to get a thorough feedback of their child.
• Conduction of Teacher’s Orientation Programme thus helping in increasing the efficiency of teachers.
• Bifurcation of the parent institution in various departments: it felicitates easy working of the organisation.
• Provision of extra-curricular activities for the students which will enhance their cultural skills as well as ethical values.
• Regular feedback sessions attained both by students, guardians and teachers.
• Issuing of notice by the highest authority thereby creating a feeling of unity and oneness among all members.
• Creating a difference in the lives of both, the students and the teachers.
